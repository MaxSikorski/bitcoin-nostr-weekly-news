# Bitcoin & Nostr Weekly News

A weekly news and discussion presentation for our Bitcoin-only meetup group. Each week we cover live Bitcoin metrics, Bitcoin market moves, regulation and policy, stablecoin news as it relates to Bitcoin, corporate treasury players, mining, and what's happening on Nostr — then open the floor for discussion.

**[View the Presentations](https://MaxSikorski.github.io/bitcoin-nostr-weekly-news)** &nbsp;·&nbsp; **[Join your Meetup](https://github.com/MaxSikorski/My-Bitcoin-Meetups/blob/main/list.md)**

---

## What This Is

Each week, a new presentation is published covering:

- **Bitcoin News** — Price and market structure, ETFs, mining, and corporate treasuries
- **Regulation & Policy** — Bills, acts, agency rules, and government reserve news
- **Stablecoins** — Where regulated digital dollars meet Bitcoin
- **Nostr** — Clients, protocol developments, zaps, and the wider ecosystem
- **Quick Tips** — Practical wallet, node, and self-custody tips worth sharing
- **Community Topics** — Whatever the group is curious about

The presentations run live at our weekly Zoom meetups and are archived [here](https://MaxSikorski.github.io/bitcoin-nostr-weekly-news) for anyone who missed one.

## Want to Share Something?

Found a wild headline, a new Nostr app, a bill moving through Congress, or a self-custody tip this week? Submit it and we'll try to cover it at the next meetup. No GitHub experience needed — it only takes a minute.

**[Submit a Topic →](https://github.com/MaxSikorski/bitcoin-nostr-weekly-news/issues/new/choose)**

### How it works

1. **Click the link above** and you'll land on the Issues page.
2. **Select the template** — click "Get started" next to *"Submit a Meetup Topic"*.
3. **Fill in the details** — the template will guide you. Replace the bracketed placeholders with your topic. Drop in any links (article, YouTube video, Nostr note, GitHub repo) and a quick description of why it's worth covering.
4. **Hit "Submit new issue"** at the bottom — that's it!

You can share anything, for example:

- A news article or policy development you spotted
- A YouTube video worth watching as a group
- A new Nostr client, relay, or feature blowing up
- A wallet, node, or Lightning tool worth demoing
- A question you want to put to the group

We read every submission and do our best to work them into the weekly lineup.
